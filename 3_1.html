Updating your status


A status is something you can post to let your friends know what's going on in your life.

At the top of the page you will see a field asking you “what’s on your mind” that looks like this:


Here, you can make a post/update your status. One you click on that field, you can type in whatever message you want. There are also options to select your current mood or what you are doing. In this example, he will be feeling ‘cute’.


Once you select your chosen mood or activity, you will see a screen giving you options to customize your post. You can change the color of the background as well as tag friends or change your privacy settings (who can see the post).
