Creating a new account


Go to Facebook's website by clicking the following link: www.facebook.com.

The following page should pop up.


Under the Sign Up section on the right side of the page, enter your personal information.


  • To enter your name, left click on the field labeled “First name” and type in your first name. Then, repeat for your last name.
  • In the field labeled “Mobile number or email,” type in either your email or your cell phone number. It does not matter which one you choose, but just remember that you will have to always enter the same email or phone number, so choose whichever one is more convenient. If you choose to enter your phone number, you can just enter the digits. (e.g. if your phone number is (123) 456-7890, you can just type in "1234567890"
  • Left click on the field labeled “New password” and type in your password (make sure your password is at least eight characters long includes at least one number one symbol and one letter and is something you will remember)
  • Under the Birthday section
  • Left click on the field displaying the current month. You should see a list of months drop down. Locate your birth month and select it.
  • Left click on the field displaying the current day of the month, locate your birthday in the new box that opened up and left click on it
  • Left click on the field displaying a year, locate your birth year in the new box that opened up and left click on it
  • Select your gender by clicking the circle next to the label.

Once you're done entering your information, hit the "Sign Up" button.

You may be asked for a confirmation code in order to complete your sign up. This code will be sent to your email or cell phone depending on which you signed up with. Wait for this code via email or text message, and enter it into the appropriate field.
